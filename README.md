# KnowIT

> **Built from curiosity, refined through experimentation.**

KnowIT is a growing collection of technical documentation covering a wide range of computing topics.

It was born from one simple habit:

> **If I had to spend hours figuring something out, I'll make sure the next person doesn't have to.**

Every guide starts with curiosity, grows through experimentation, and ends as documentation that aims to be clear, practical, and approachable.

The goal isn't to replace official documentation. It's to bridge the gap between official references and real understanding by explaining not only **what** something is, but also **why** it works the way it does.

Think of it less as a textbook and more as the notes you'd wish someone had handed you before you started.

---

## Who is KnowIT for?

Everyone.

Whether you're discovering a topic for the first time, revisiting one you haven't touched in years, or simply looking for a quick reminder, KnowIT is designed to help you understand—not just memorize.

This repository is intended for:

* **Beginners** learning a concept from the ground up.
* **Students** who want explanations that prioritize understanding.
* **Professionals** who occasionally need a refresher.
* **Curious minds** who simply enjoy learning how things work.

---

## How is the documentation organized?

KnowIT follows a simple principle:

> **One topic, one document.**

Each document focuses on a single subject and includes its own table of contents for quick navigation.

The documentation follows a logical flow of thought, introducing concepts progressively while remaining useful as a quick reference later on.
